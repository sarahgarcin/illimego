Summary:

Located in the heart of Nièvre in Burgundy, the boarding school of the Dominican Teaching Sisters of Franjeaux has an exceptional site on the edge of the Bois de la Rivière.

This school of around 30 students now intends to expand in the coming years to accommodate a greater number of students ranging from elementary school to crafts high school for.

The estate has several buildings including a castle surrounded by a moat which is undergoing complete restructuring in order to enhance the old heritage and accommodate the dormitories of the boarding school.
